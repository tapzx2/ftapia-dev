Location Shoots

Projects are bid on a job-by-job basis. Project costs are based on the type of job and the time needed to complete a shoot. We understand that our clients prefer to know a fixed cost in advance. We bid most of our projects on a flat fee basis to keep the process as simple and predictable as possible.

Product Shoots

Products using translucent light table (produces pure white background) or 4x8 ft. tabletop (use seamless paper and produces a shadow).

Price for single product using simlar lighting, framing and styling are $50 each with a minimum of 5 images.

Produces with relective or translucent surfaces and a group of produces are treated as unique products shots, thus prices varies. Call for a quote.
